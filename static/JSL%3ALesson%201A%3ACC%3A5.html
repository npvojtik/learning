Conversation 5

N checks with the secretary on whether some work has been completed.

出来ます has a number of different meanings depending on context. In reference to a project it refers to completion. It may also refer to personal ability or capability or possibility. When used of languages, it refers to proficiency.

出来ましたできましたかか✓。
All done?
出来ましたできました。はいはい。
