Conversation 9

J checks on his assumption that his colleague N attended an event advertised on a poster they are looking at.

行きます refers to motion away from the speaker or locations connected with the speaker. In contrast, 来ます refers to motion toward the speaker.

行きましたいきましたねね⸮。
You went, right?
いいえ、行きませんいきませんでした。
No, I didn't (go).
